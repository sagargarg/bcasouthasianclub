Diwali Dinner Dance 2014

It's that time of the year again!! South Asian Club's Annual Diwali Dinner Dance is on Friday November 21 from 7:00pm to 10:00pm in the gymnasium! There will be a live DJ, a full catered meal, henna, dancing, a photobooth, and much more!! Buy your tickets starting Monday, November 17 during lunch mods in the upper cafeteria or from Dr. Bajwa (Room 146).

Tickets

Tickets are $10 from November 17-18, $12 from November 19-21, and $15 at the door. Buy them before they sell out!


All proceeds will go to SAHAITA.
