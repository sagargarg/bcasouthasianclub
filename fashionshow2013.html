South Asian Club

Fashion Show 2012

The South Asian culture is known for its ornate and elaborate style as well as its extravagant clothing. Students from all cultures and background dressed up in saris, salwars, and kurtas to showcase the beautiful fashion of the South Asian culture. We even had some teachers and to top off the event were some snacks and a 'Desi Girl' flashmob!
