Holi 2014

Holi is the spring festival of colors, love, and friendship. Holi festivities mark the beginning of new year to many Indians, as well as a justification to reset and renew ruptured relationships, end conflicts and accumulated emotional impurities from past. At this event we had a dodgeball tournament with water balloons, the traditional holi ceremony as well as some outdoor snacks!
