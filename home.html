South Asian Club

A cultural and service oriented organization

Mission Statement

The South Asian Club at BCA aims to raise funds for a charity while promoting cultural awareness at the Bergen County Academies. We hold multiple events throughout the year to raise money for Sahaita. Sahaita is a non-profit organization committed to educating, supporting, and uplifting the underprivileged members of society. They accomplish this through healthcare initiatives, education sponsorships, vocational training and affiliated partner non-profit organizations. South Asian Club donates 100% of its funds to Sahaita. In the past, we have held South Asian themed cultural nights, fashion shows, dances, and festive celebrations. To get involved join us for our weekly meetings in Dr. Bajwa's room (Rm. 147) every Thursday morning at 7:40!
